ARERA Smart
2024
UX and UI design
System design
Make energy easy
ARERA Smart was designed to meet the growing need for households to better understand their energy consumption and navigate their often complex bills. By leveraging the 2G Smart Meter and AI technology, ARERA Smart not only allows users to easily monitor their energy usage but also predicts future consumption, offering insights into how much energy they are likely to use. This makes managing energy efficient and straightforward, transforming bill reading into an engaging and even enjoyable experience.
At the end of each month, users can access SmartBill, a concise summary of their energy bill. Through simple and intuitive comparisons, they can easily see if their consumption stayed below the AI forecast, earning them recognition as an "excellent consumer" for the month. To visually represent how efficiently users are consuming, Homie—the app’s mascot—was created. Homie changes color and expression to reflect the user's consumption efficiency, making it easy and engaging to track progress in a fun, visual way.
Real-time energy consumption
The goal of ARERA Smart is to make energy awareness accessible to everyone. The home screen continuously displays real-time energy consumption, along with how much the user is saving compared to the AI's monthly forecast, encouraging more efficient energy use. This clear, actionable insight motivates users to consume less and manage their energy more wisely.
Within the app, users can choose their preferred unit of measurement, either in kWh or euros, simplifying the data while tailoring it to individual preferences. In the Peers section, users can compare their consumption in kWh against others with similar lifestyles, offering valuable context for understanding their own energy use.
To further gamify savings, users can also compare their consumption with friends and family, creating a fun, competitive environment to encourage mutual improvement. In the Analytics section, users gain detailed insights into their consumption, broken down by macro-categories and individual appliances, helping them identify which devices are driving up their bills.
Double win
With Homie and simplified information management, ARERA Smart empowers users to easily understand their energy consumption, learn to read their bills, and make smarter choices. By saving energy, they not only reduce their costs but also contribute to protecting the planet.
