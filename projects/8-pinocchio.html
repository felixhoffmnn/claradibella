Pinocchio Revenge
The story of Pinocchio has been reworked to make it a shooter video game. In this version of the story Mangiafuoco kills Geppetto, enraging Pinocchio who craves revenge. In the videogame, the user as Pinocchio must traverse 3 levels: the village, Toyland, and finally Mangiafuoco's circus with the final boss.
Puppets showdown
The game is a typical platform video game, where the user has to shoot at 3 different enemies (the Mangiafuoco's puppets) to defend himself. Life and ammo can be recharged by collecting objects along the way. At each level, progress is saved.
Final boss
In the final level, Pinocchio faces Mangiafuoco, the ultimate boss. After defeating him, the player receives a score based on time, damage taken, and enemies defeated, encouraging replayability and improving performance with each playthrough.
Story mode
From the game’s home page, players can explore the tale of Pinocchio's Revenge through immersive 3D renderings, enriching the gameplay experience with the narrative behind the action.
