Factanza is a media company aiming to revolutionize the world of information by making it more engaging, youthful, and accessible. With a community of over a million users across social media and digital platforms, Factanza delivers informative content on current events and key topics to help people better understand the world and its changes.
During my internship at the company, I was in charge of the graphic design of content for social media, under the supervision of the senior graphic designers. It was an enriching learning experience that gave me valuable insight into the workflow of an editorial team.
